A little about me

I arrived in Wellington in November of 2008 from sunny California where I used to work as a machinist creating airline parts, both manually on the old-fashioned manual machines, and by programming parts in CAD for creating in CNC (computer numerical control) machining.

What is CNC machining? Most people I speak to have an image in their heads of a factory or an assembly line with a fellow pressing a button, stamping out parts over and over again. Sometimes it’s hard to stress just how incorrect this misconception is. If you’re curious about how cool CNC machining really is, and what it can do, check out this video and be amazed.
